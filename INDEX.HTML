Melly Mel & Co LLC Operating Protocol

I. Continuity of Service

To ensure the integrity of your logistics, all service agreements operate on an automatic month-to-month renewal cycle. This enables Effficiency Scheduling Services to maintain long-term data consistency and spreadsheet accuracy without administrative lapse.

II. Resolution Protocol

Melly Mel & Co LLC mandates a 30-day good faith negotiation period for all operational adjustments or disputes. We prioritize direct, professional dialogue to resolve hurdles, maintaining the "Triple-F" (Fast, Focused, Functional) standard at all times.

III. Transition Flexibility

We support modern business agility with a standard 14-day termination notice. This window ensures a clean handoff of spreadsheets, calendar access, and dispatch logs, protecting your business continuity.
